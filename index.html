About me

A lifelong practice built around you.

I came to Classical Pilates after years of pushing my body too hard and recovering from an injury from the demands of Pole training. The practice changed everything — not just physically, but in the way I move through daily life.

Now I teach because I want that for everyone. Whether you're recovering from injury, building strength or better posture that lasts a lifetime — I meet you exactly where you are.
